Stormcloak Seasoning

This is one of the most popular seasoning mixed across the breadth of Skyrim, where nearly every garden boasts a little corner dedicated to grow most of these ingredients. The grains of paradise are imported, and if the cost is too dear, ordinary black pepper can be substituted. It makes for a mixture that goes well with vegetables, fish, meat, and savory baking.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons dried dill
  • 1/2 teaspoon grains of paradise
  • 1 teaspoon mustard powder
  • 2 teaspoon fennel seeds

Steps

  1. Crush or grind all the ingredients together using a mortar and pestle or a clean spice grinder, then store in a small airtight jar.
